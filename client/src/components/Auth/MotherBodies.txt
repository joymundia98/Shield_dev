The **four major church mother bodies in Zambia** are:

1. **Zambia Conference of Catholic Bishops (ZCCB)**
   – Represents the **Roman Catholic Church** in Zambia.

2. **Council of Churches in Zambia (CCZ)**
   – An ecumenical body mainly representing **mainline Protestant churches** (e.g., United Church of Zambia, Anglican Church).

3. **Evangelical Fellowship of Zambia (EFZ)**
   – Represents **evangelical, Pentecostal, and charismatic churches**.

4. **Independent Churches of Zambia (ICOZ)**
   – Represents **African Independent/Indigenous churches**.

These four bodies are widely recognized by the government and often speak collectively on national, social, and moral issues.
